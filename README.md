## Introduction  
The DJI Mavic 4 Pro Creator Combo is a professional-grade aerial filmmaking powerhouse designed for creators who demand exceptional image quality and unmatched creative freedom. Central to its performance is a groundbreaking triple-camera system featuring a 100MP Hasselblad 4/3 CMOS sensor, complemented by 48MP medium telephoto and 50MP long telephoto lenses. This allows seamless switching between expansive wide shots, dynamic portraits, and precise long-range photography without landing or changing lenses. With support for 6K/60fps HDR video, 10-bit color depth, and up to 16 stops of dynamic range, the Mavic 4 Pro captures breathtakingly detailed footage and images with studio-level precision.

## Design & Build  
The drone boasts a sleek, foldable design that combines portability with durability. Equipped with omnidirectional obstacle avoidance enhanced by LiDAR and night vision sensors operating down to 0.1 lux, it ensures safe and confident flying in even the most challenging environments, including night-time or urban settings. The Infinity Gimbal supports full 360° rotation and 70° upward tilt, opening new possibilities for creative camera movements such as orbital shots and vertical reveals.

## Performance  
Equipped with a 95Wh battery, the Mavic 4 Pro delivers up to 51 minutes of flight time and a max speed of 90 kph. It utilizes the DJI O4+ 10-bit HDR transmission system that provides ultra-stable live video with a range up to 30 km, ideal for precise control during complex shoots. The internal 512GB SSD enables seamless high-bitrate recording in ProRes and All-I formats, minimizing downtime and speeding up post-production workflows.

## Control & Connectivity  
The included DJI RC Pro 2 controller features a 7-inch ultra-bright 2000-nit Mini-LED display supporting HDMI output and a smart folding design for ergonomic use. This advanced control station integrates tightly with the drone’s capabilities, providing real-time telemetry, camera control, and customizable controls perfect for professional production.

## Use Cases  
Whether shooting cinematic films, commercial advertisements, documentaries, or travel content, the Mavic 4 Pro Creator Combo equips filmmakers with a versatile, reliable tool that elevates aerial storytelling. Its extensive feature set supports creative exploration in diverse scenarios, from sweeping landscapes to intimate close-ups and low-light environments.

## Package  
The combo includes the drone, DJI RC Pro 2 controller, three intelligent flight batteries, a parallel charging hub, multiple ND filters, spare propellers, a storage cover, and a shoulder bag, ensuring professional readiness to shoot all day.
